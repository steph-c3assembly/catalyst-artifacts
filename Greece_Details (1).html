Youth (ages 12–17)
Ozone Teen Zone · Porto Sani

Teens aged 12–17 join Ozone, located at Porto Sani. No pre-booking required — just head over on arrival and sign the registration form, which grants them permission to sign themselves in and out independently.

A weekly activity timetable is posted on-site. Teens browse it and the team reserves them a spot in their chosen activities. Flexible and self-directed.
